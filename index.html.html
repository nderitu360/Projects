About Us

Riritech Africa is a forward-thinking tech company focused on driving innovation across the African continent. We specialize in software solutions, AI development, and digital transformation strategies tailored for emerging markets.Founded by Riri, RiriTech is a community-focused initiative driving tech education and environmental sustainability. We equip youth, women, and rural innovators with digital skills and climate-tech knowledge to build a better Africa.



Our mission

Riritech Africa is committed to delivering cutting-edge technology solutions that enhance efficiency, foster economic growth, and improve lives. Through strategic partnerships, research, and sustainable practices, we aim to bridge the digital divide and create impactful technological advancements tailored to the African market.

Our vision

To be the leading technology solutions provider in Africa, driving innovation, empowering communities, and shaping a digitally connected future.
