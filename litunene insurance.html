Our history

Litunene Insurance Agency was founded in 2019, Over the years, we have earned the trust and loyalty of over 1.9 million clients across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, and Mauritius, thanks to our dedication to creating insurance solutions with passion and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
